Work Placement

Overview

The third semester of the course requires us to complete 3 months of work experience with a business or company in our field of study to gain practical experience in the work place.

Continuous Assessment

There isn't particular projects but you must submit a monthly newsletter to explain how you are getting on and tasks you have completed and things you have learned.
