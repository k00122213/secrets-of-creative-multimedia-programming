Semesters

This one year course is broken up into 3 different semesters. This course is one of the few that have started with the semesterized schedule in the Limerick Institute of Technology and something I have head they will be hoping to apply to almost all courses in the future. The courses are broken up between September to December, from January to May and then, because our course requires a work placement module we have the third semester in Summer from June to the end of August.

On this page I have broken down what subjects are covered in each semester aand have links to their individual pages with more detail about that subject.

Semester 1

Web Programming, Web Development, 3D Graphics, Digital Video, Multimedia Database Systems and Design and Interactive Design.

Semester 2

Evolving Web Technologies, Advanced Web Programming, Interactive Interface Design, Multimedia Project and electives such as Human Cognition and Communication or Project Management

Semester 3

Work Placement
