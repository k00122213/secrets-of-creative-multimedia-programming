Multimedia Project

Overview

The entire module is a final year multimedia project. Its objectives for 2016/17 were determined by the RSA's Student Design Awards Briefs. There were many to choose from . I chose Number 12 called 'Moving Pictures' that involved creating an animation and a website to feature the animation.

Continuous Assessment

The entire module is one big project but there are deadlines and deliverables such as a proposal report, research report, presentations and demonstrations. You are also required to complete a report.

Links to blogs/websites

As 10% of our grade is allocated to blogging about our process, I maintain mine every time i finish something new. You can visit my blogger account to view my progress. It is still currently being updated.

Insiders Hints and Tips

Timing is everything with this module. Try and accurately determine how long tasks will take. Also, choose a project that interests you. You will spend so much time doing it so its best to do something you enjoy or make something about what you enjoy.
